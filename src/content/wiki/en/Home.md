# Akku-Craft: Sustainable · Modular · Decentralized

Welcome to the core documentation of Akku-Craft. We are building a future where battery systems are no longer disposable black boxes — our mission is to reduce electronic waste through a modular battery ecosystem with decentralized monitoring.

Instead of discarding an entire battery pack because a single cell fails, Akku-Craft enables repair, scalability, and granular control over every component.

## The Vision

The current battery industry is driven by planned obsolescence and proprietary designs. Akku-Craft breaks this cycle through three core pillars:

1. **Sustainable** — Designed for repairability and long service life.
2. **Modular** — Scale capacity by adding units, not by redesigning the system.
3. **Decentralized** — Intelligence is distributed across the system for improved safety and reliability.

## How It Works

Akku-Craft is a synergy of three main domains:

- **Hardware** ([Schematics](wiki/schematics)): Circuit logic and power distribution.
- **Mechanics** ([3D Models](wiki/mechanical-design)): The housing that keeps everything safe and portable.
- **Firmware** ([ArduinoBMS](wiki/firmware-arduinobms)): The brain that monitors voltage, current, and temperature to ensure safe operation.
